FAQ

Learn a little bit more about Dovera Insights and customer reputation from these frequently asked questions.

My free trial ended, now what?

At the end of your 14-day free trial you will be able to select which plan best fits your needs.

What is the refund policy?

You may cancel your subscription at any time, however, it will be valid until the end of your current subscription.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

If you cancel your subscription all third party reviews will remain and you will be able to export your review data.
