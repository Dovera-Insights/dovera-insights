Release 1.0.4

Our first release of the year with many great features to help streamline gathering customer feedback and online reviews.

Features:
  • Dashboard with charts
  • Ability to create a campaign
  • Add or upload bulk customers
  • Update business information
  • Toggle globally review links
Bug Fixes:
  • Fixed issue with URL shortening not working
  • Fixed email and text counts clearing too early
  • Fixed an issue where users were getting an oops screen when saving a review
