Examples of the information we collect and analyze in this manner include the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your Equipment to the Internet; your browser type and version, operating system, and platform; your activities on our Website, including the URL you come from and go to next (whether this URL is on our site or not); and cookie number. If you are concerned about the storage and use of cookies, you may be able to delete cookies manually from your hard drive or mobile device or take other steps to prevent cookies from loading in the first place.
